Our Matcha-Making Process

At Raya's Matcha Store, we take pride in crafting the perfect cup of matcha. Our process begins with sourcing the highest quality shade-grown tea leaves, which are carefully stone-ground into a fine, vibrant green powder. Each serving is then meticulously prepared using traditional tools like the chasen (bamboo whisk) and chawan (matcha bowl). We sift the matcha to ensure a smooth texture, whisk it with precision, and serve it with love. Whether you're enjoying a classic matcha or one of our specialty creations, every sip is a celebration of tradition and flavor.
