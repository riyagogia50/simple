Our Matcha-Making Process

At Raya's Matcha Store, we take pride in crafting the perfect cup of matcha. Our process begins with sourcing the highest-quality shade-grown tea leaves, which are carefully stone-ground into a fine, vibrant green powder. Each serving is then meticulously prepared using traditional tools like the chasen (bamboo whisk) and chawan (matcha bowl) to ensure a smooth, frothy texture. Whether you're enjoying a classic matcha or one of our signature specialties, every sip is a celebration of flavor and tradition.
